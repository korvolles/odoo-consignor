Consignor Shipment Server

With Consignor for Odoo, you can now registre and print all your deliveries in ONE system.
Functionality

  • Check shipping price on Quote/Sales order
  • Submit Shipping Order
  • Tracking reference
  • Shipping labels saved on document

Access to over 300 Shipping Companies

Consignor supports over 300 shipping companies in the nordic countries, of those aproximately 70 are located in Norway. You get access to all of their products and services. Consignor handles all of the messaging between you and the shipping company you choose.

Odoo & Consignor Configuration

After installation of the Consignor Module a new Delivery Method is created called Consignor.

  • Server URL: Insert the Consignor Shipment Server address
  • Insert the KEY you obtained from Consignor setting up your account
  • Insert the ACCOUNT ID you obtained from Consignor setting up your account
  • Select the Product Category where the Consignor Shipping Products and services will be registered under
  • Enabling test mode will fetch the information about your Cosnignor account, but not update your Odoo installation
  • Press the Update Consignor Information button to update your system with your shipping partners and their products and services

When configuration is done.
Shipping suppliers and shipping products has been created in your system along with new delivery methods.
